Pumpkin Soup

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin - 1kg
  • garlic - 2 cloves
  • Onion - 1
  • Celery - 2 stick
  • olive oil - splash
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Peel and cut pumpkin into large pieces. finely dices onion, garlic and celery.
  2. Fry onoins and celery for 5 min and garlic for 1 before adding pumpkin for another 5 minutes.
  3. Add 2 litres water and simmer for 1.5 hours
  4. add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Serve and enjoy
